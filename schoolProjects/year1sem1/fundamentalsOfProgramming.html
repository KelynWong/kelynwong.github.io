MCQ quiz

This project is one of the my first program I've created. It is a simple MCQ quiz that is made purely by javascript. I used what I've learnt in class which includes arrays, functions and loop to create this. To view the codes click here.

How it works.

Due to only using javascript, I used console.log() to display the quiz. When you first run the program, this is what you would see. An introduction and instruction for the quiz and a prompt to enter your name.

After entering your name, you will be prompted to choose the category of quiz. There are four option for you to choose! Jokes, odd one out, online slang and minecraft.

This is what would be shown for each question. In this case, this is the question for the jokes category. You would be prompted to enter the answer for the question. If you are unsure, you have the option to go to the next question for entering 'N'. You would also have an option to go to the previous question using 'P' and 'X' to submit.

Once you have completed all questions, you will be shown a summary page of the questions and your answers. You are then able to check through and if you want to change your answer for a particular question, you can enter the question number. Otherwise, enter 0 to submit your answers.

After you submitted, the scores would be calculated for you and you would be shown your results. There will be different messages displayed depending on how much your have scored.
