Critical Analytical Thinking

Soft skills

This module has taught me how to think in a analytical way. Due to the ease of being able to access all sorts of information online, we need to learn how to sift out information that is trustable and reliable to us. For this project, there is two parts. The first part consist of researching on a topic that we as a team of 5 would like to work on and being interviewed on what we have researched, proving that the data that we have gathered is indeed reliable. For the second part of the project, we had to continue on part one where we improved on it based on the feedback that we gotten. Furthermore, we had to pick a stand (FOR or AGAINST) for the topic we have at hand and present it to the class.

Part 1.

My team and I brainstormed to came up with several topics. We decided to go with the third one which is "Should gay marraiges be legalized?".

Each member of the team is then assigned to do research on both FOR (gay marraies should be legalized) or AGAINST (gay marraies should NOT be legalized).

For each article/video we found relating to our topic had to go through a tool that we learnt called "CRAAP". To view the document click here.

Part 2.

Due to feedback from the lecturer, we changed our topic from "Should gay marraiges be legalized" to "Should plastics be banned?". We took a stand of FOR (plastics should be banned).

Based on our research, we had to give the reasons on why we stand banning of plastics.
