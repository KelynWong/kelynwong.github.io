Communication For Project Effectiveness

Soft skills

In this module we had to think of a relevant issue that is happening in Singapore. In a team of 3, we then have to come out with a proposed solution to the issue and write a full report on the proposed solution. The report have to consist of the background of the issue, proposed solution, how to publicise the public on the solution and how the solution is to be implemented. Besides learning how to write the report, we had to learn how to present our solution in a formal and conviencing way.

Part 1.

The situation that we were given is that we are writing a report for the National Youth Council (NYC) for the Young ChangeMakers (YCM) grant.

Our team choose an issue in Singapore which is "Food Wastage" as we found that it is quite a prominent issue in Singapore.
