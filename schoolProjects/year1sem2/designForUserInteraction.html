A booking app for students and lecturers

For this project, it's all about designing an application in the best way to suit the users. In our group of 3, we came out with an application idea for students (targeted users) to book their lectures for consultations. We decided to pick this idea out of all the ideas we have came up with because we personally face the issue of not being able to get a consultation with our lecturer as well due to not understanding the lecturer's schedule. To view the other app ideas click here.

The project is split into three parts. Part one consists of conducting interviews, making a journey map, persona, sitemap and wireframes of the application. Second part is making a prototype and letting our previously interviewed users try out our application then we recorded our observation on how they interacted with our application and their feedback. Lastly, we move on to improving our wireframes and prototype based on the user's feedback. Also, besides the phone application we created the ipad version layout for our application.

Part 1.

To start off with the project, we interviewed our targeted users which in our case is students. Going around the school, each of us in the team found one student to interview with. After gaining insights from our interviewees, we are then able to craft out the user persona and a journey map. From this, we make the wireframes to design our application just for our users with careful considerations like button sizes, positioning of elements and even colours.

To view the project folder for part 1 click here.

Part 2.

For this second part of the project, we created the prototype (linking of the wireframes together when button is pressed) and gave our users that we interview eariler to test it out. We video recorded how the user interacted with our prototype and their facial expressions for later reviewing to note down our observations of the user interaction. We also gave our user a survey as feedback for us to know how well designed the application is for the user. Besides that, we refined our sitemap and wireframes.

To view the project folder for part 2 click here.

Part 3.

Last part of the project includes creating a layout design for ipad users. A whole new set of wireframe is produced. Additionally, we annotated the wireframes and arranged the wireframes in the way to show how the application flow of events.
